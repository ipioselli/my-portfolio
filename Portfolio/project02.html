02 Cafe Website Design

UI/UX Design

Design System

Role: UI/UX designer, Graphic Designer

Project type: UI Design

Duration: 4 weeks

Tools: Adobe XD, Illustrator

Overview

This is a personal project I worked on for a virtual cafe. I combined my love for coffee and design to create an aesthetically pleasing cafe website. With meticulous attention to detail and a dedication to aesthetic excellence, I crafted a website design that not only showcases the allure of coffee culture but also captivates visitors with its visual charm using Adobe XD and Illustrator.

XD Prototype

Problem Space

How can we design a visually engaging and user-friendly cafe website that effectively showcases our menu, reflects our brand identity and storytelling, and provides seamless navigation and contact options for our customers?

For the website design, it is important to create an appealing and visually engaging website that reflects the ambiance and branding of the cafe. This includes selecting appropriate colors, fonts, imagery, and layout to evoke the desired atmosphere. It is also important to showcase the cafe's unique branding, story, and values. Use compelling storytelling and visuals to connect with customers on a deeper level and differentiate the cafe from competitors.

Goal

My goal for this project is to create a unique and intuitive design system for the cafe and ensure that the website offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for visitors. I will rely on competitive analysis research to understand the cafe lovers needs as well as to understand what functions they want in a website.

Solution

My solution for this project is to create a cafe brand from my research and make a design system showcasing colour explorations, typography and logo design. With that I will be able to create my cafe website prototype using Adobe Xd and demonstrate the user experience of the website.

Research

Research

In order to design a cafe brand and website I needed to conduct research on existing cafe's to understand the market and see what works and what doesn't.I researched many different successful cafes in Montreal and went through their websites and analyzed each of their sections to understand the UI and what to incorporate into my own design.
