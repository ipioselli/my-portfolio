UI/UX Design

Research

Role: UI/UX designer, Researcher

Project type: App Prototype

Duration: 4 weeks

Tools: Figma, Procreate, Photoshop

Overview

What is your carbon footprint?

The amount of greenhouse gasses released into the atmosphere caused by human activities. The greenhouse gasses trap heat in the atmosphere, which accelerates the heating of the globe. I wanted to explore the environmental impact of our carbon footprint on climate change and how we can offset our individual carbon footprint. I wanted to raise awareness on this topic through an app prototype that uses gamification elements to enhance user engagement.

Problem Space

How can gamification and the use of “living” animals be used to engage user’s to take positive actions towards climate change and experience their carbon footprint?

Climate change and carbon footprint can be challenging topics to digest. I aimed to explore alternative methods to engage users in this subject. Many applications leverage gamification features to encourage users to remain engaged, as seen in DuoLingo. I sought to delve deeper into the intersection of gamification features, user engagement, and climate change in my research.

Goal

My goal for this project was to create an intuitive design that meets the needs of the users and the target audience by taking my research into account. I want to include gamification features to increase user engagement and implement simple tasks for users to complete in order for them to be more conscious of their individual carbon footprint.

Solution

My solution to this problem was to design an interactive app by conducting research on existing products and conducting user interviews. I also wanted to create an interactive prototype using gamification and tamagotchi game elements like in Duo Lingo and PokemonGo and see how user's react to climate change when they have the responsibility of taking care of a "living" creature

Research

Research Plan

For my research I looked at existing successful gamified apps such as Duo Lingo and Pokemon Go and decided I would center my research creation around that. Through academic research, I discovered that having a virtual pet/Tamagotchi creates an emotional attachment between the user and objects. User's are also more likely to keep using an app if they know that a life is in their responsibility. Gamification adds a motivational and competitive element, gives the user control and a feeling of achievement. I conduced user testing interviews after the prototype was done to receive feedback.

Research Paper

User Questions

My target audience for my user research was young people and universtiy students and I conducted a short interview with 4 users. I asked them questions to understand how they feel about gamified apps and to gather their understanding and knoweledhe on climate change and carbon footprint.

Plan

The main concept of this app is to help people offset their carbon footprint by taking care of a cute pet I wanted to include:

  • Points after completing a task that helps offset your carbon footprint
  • A streak system to encourage user's to take care of their pet
  • Have a leaderboard to see friends and other user's points
